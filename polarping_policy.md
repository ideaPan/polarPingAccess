## 1. Information Collection and Use
SantaApp is designed for family use. The application is downloaded and managed by a parent or guardian, who may permit their child to interact with the application’s automated chatbot feature. 

### On-Device Processing & Chat Data
* **No Server Syncing:** To ensure complete privacy, SantaApp does not transmit, upload, or sync chat conversations, inputs, or history to any external servers. All text interactions and chatbot processing happen locally on the user's device.
* **No Account Creation:** We do not require children or parents to create an account or provide names, emails, or personal details to use the chatbot.
* **Complete Parent Control:** All local chat history is fully accessible to the parent and can be deleted entirely at any time directly through the app’s settings or by clearing the app's local storage.

## 3. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K Compliance)
Because SantaApp is intended for family use and may be handled by children, we take extra precautions to protect young users:
* We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in the UK/EU/US).
* We do not use behavioral tracking, targeted advertising, or harvest persistent device identifiers from child users.
* Since all data remains strictly on the local device, we hold zero child data on any external servers.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion
All data generated by SantaApp remains on your device until the app is uninstalled or you manually clear the app's data. Parents have the absolute right to delete all interaction history at any point.
